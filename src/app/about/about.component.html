Vission

Indian Cooperation

India's difficulty is its unclean society, particularly its water, sanitation, and drainage infrastructure. Within four to five days, the appropriate worker may arrive to clean that location. Until then, these wastes may spread several diseases through the air and animals, as well as degrade the environment. When the appropriate person doesn't show up, the society becomes increasingly unclean. India generates between 0.2 and 0.6 pounds of trash per person every day. We are all accountable for the development of India as citizens. We suggested the creation of an Android application as a solution to this environmental problem.

Mission

PM & CM

This application primarily provides details on social programmes, laws that must be upheld to maintain public order, activities to be performed, and the current standing of previous complaints. If the complaint is not addressed within the allotted time, you can file another one, in which case the complaint will be forwarded to a higher authority. After some time has passed since the complaint was posted, the re-complaint option will be enabled. The use of this application is both urban and rural.

Friendly

We People

The primary goal of our application is to maintain social order. So the complaint session is the primary component. When we click the complaint session link, a login prompt appears. After providing the necessary geolocation information and user details, we must select the relevant department (water, sanitation, garbage, and drainage management), after which we must upload a photo or a video of that department. The appropriate company is then notified of the complaint, and someone will be dispatched to clean the area. If your complaint wasn't addressed within the allotted time, you can re-complain and have the message forwarded to a higher authority. This higher authority will take the necessary actions to tidy it up.
